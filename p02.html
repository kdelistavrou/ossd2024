Summer in the city

I love very much the sea and swimming. Today the sun shines. I will take one of the ferries that depart from the harbour of Thessaloniki, to visit one of the nearby beaches of Thermaikos. I will not use my car, so that I will not have to fill it with gas. I'll also hold an umbrella, in case clouds gather and it rains. I will not carry my mobile, to have peace of mind. At night I will return with the last ferry, to watch the football game for the cup on the television.
